About Us


We are the leading Rolling Mill Machinery Manufacturer in light and heavy size automatic and semi-automatic rolling mills in India. We are experts in total turn-key projects for Bar & Wire Rod Mills and Structural Mills.

We have a strong, in-house service department which meets the day-to-day service requirements of our customers, even after the complete erection and commissioning of the steel plant.

The project from beginning to end is completed under our supervision and a client has to just describe the capacity requirement. We have skilled personnel working under us and each and every work is completed within the given deadlines. From civil work to shed, EOT Cranes and electrical work, each part is handled by us. This leaves our client free from any type of stress and we can do the same work with a comparatively lesser cost because we specialize in this area and therefore know how to save the precious money and time of the clients.

We consider it our responsibility to provide you the desired production result within the promised timeframe as per your requirement
